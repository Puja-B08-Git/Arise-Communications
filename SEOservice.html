Your Questions Answered.

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It’s the practice of optimizing your website to improve its visibility in search engines like Google, Bing, Yahoo, helping drive organic (non-paid) traffic to your site.

There are three main types: On-page SEO (optimizing content and HTML), Off-page SEO (backlink building and social signals), and Technical SEO (improving site structure, speed, and crawlability).

SEO is a long-term strategy. While some improvements can be seen in a few weeks, significant results often take 3 to 6 months depending on your industry, competition, and strategy.
